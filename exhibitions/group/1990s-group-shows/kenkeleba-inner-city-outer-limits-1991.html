Notes

Inner City / Outer Limits was a six-artist exhibition at Kenkeleba Gallery on Manhattan’s Lower East Side, featuring Andrew Castrucci, Carlos “Waki” Rodriguez, Lee Quinones, Sandra “Pink” Fabara, Ron English, and Martin Wong.

The exhibition brought together artists whose work grew from urban street culture, graffiti, political critique, commercial imagery, and narrative realism. In this context, Ron English’s paintings extended his satirical engagement with American media, advertising, and political culture into a gallery setting.

Contemporary coverage connected the show to the atmosphere of the early 1990s, including war imagery, media saturation, and the psychological pressures of urban life. English’s contribution fit within that broader examination of inner-city experience and pop-cultural critique.
