Notes

5th Annual Metro Show was the fifth edition of the Metro Show presented by Newark’s Urban Artists' Collective, also known as City Without Walls. The exhibition brought together regional artists whose work engaged with social, cultural, and urban themes.

Ron English participated in this 1986 exhibition during a period when independent and experimental artists in the New York and New Jersey area were gaining wider visibility through collaborative group shows and alternative art spaces.

The exhibition also reflects the broader exchange between emerging visual artists working across disciplines in the mid-1980s, linking English’s early exhibition history to the urban contexts that informed his later public interventions and Popaganda imagery.
