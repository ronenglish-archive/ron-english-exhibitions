Notes

Translations was a solo exhibition at Allouche Gallery in Los Angeles presenting new paintings and sculptural works by Ron English. The show continued his POPaganda language through evolving characters, visual codes, and hybrid compositions.

The exhibition emphasized transformation across forms and symbols, pushing familiar motifs into new configurations while maintaining English’s sharp interest in branding, satire, and cultural mutation.

As a 2024 Los Angeles presentation, the exhibition is a useful record of how English continued to develop his visual vocabulary through both painting and sculpture in a tightly focused gallery setting.
