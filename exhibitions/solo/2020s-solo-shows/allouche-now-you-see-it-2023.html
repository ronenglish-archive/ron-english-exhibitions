Notes

Now You See It was a solo exhibition at Allouche Gallery presenting recent paintings, installations, and POPaganda imagery that extended Ron English’s investigations into perception, illusion, and layered cultural symbolism.

The exhibition emphasized optical shifts, recurring characters, and partially hidden visual structures, using both painting and sculptural forms to explore the unstable line between what appears on the surface and what operates underneath.

As a 2023 New York presentation, the show is a useful record of how English continued to evolve his visual language through perceptual play, dense iconography, and immersive gallery staging during the early 2020s.
