Notes

Texas: Naturally Surreal was a solo presentation at Ro2 Art Gallery in Dallas that framed Ron English’s POPaganda language through a Texas-inflected surrealist lens.

The exhibition brought together hybrid characters, painterly distortions, and symbolic fusions of landscape and mascot imagery, linking regional mythologies with English’s established universe of altered icons and cultural satire.

As a 2024 Dallas exhibition, the show is a useful record of how English adapted his visual language to local context while maintaining the strange humor, mutation, and commercial critique that define his broader body of work.
