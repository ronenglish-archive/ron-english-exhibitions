Notes

The Secret History of Beacon Bigfoot was a large-scale solo project at KuBe Art Center in Beacon built around a fictionalized local Bigfoot mythology. Ron English used the figure as a vehicle for parody, folklore reinvention, and POPaganda world-building.

The exhibition combined paintings, sculptural elements, and installation work, turning a regional legend into a playful but layered visual narrative. That approach let English connect upstate lore, pop-cultural absurdity, and his recurring interest in invented icon systems.

As a 2022 Beacon presentation, the show is a useful record of how English extended his practice into place-based mythmaking while keeping the satirical and theatrical charge of his broader body of work.
