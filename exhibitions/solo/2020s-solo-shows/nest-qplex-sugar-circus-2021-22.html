Notes

Sugar Circus was an immersive solo exhibition at The Nest, Q Plex in Shenzhen built around candy-culture imagery, large-scale installations, and hyper-color environments. The presentation turned the venue into a saturated POPaganda world shaped by spectacle, sweetness, and sensory overload.

Ron English extended his recurring critique of consumption, desire, youth marketing, and pop-surreal excess by framing the exhibition as a circus-like environment rather than a standard wall-based display. This approach linked the show to his broader interest in immersive visual storytelling and symbolic world-building.

As a 2021–2022 Shenzhen presentation, the exhibition is a useful record of how English continued to expand his POPaganda language through large-format installation and international exhibition design in the early 2020s.
