Notes

Delusionville presented Ron English’s latest studio paintings and sculptural works at Allouche Gallery in New York City during the fall of 2018. The exhibition centered on the expanding mythos of Delusionville, a recurring imaginative world within English’s larger Popaganda universe.

A collaborative sub-section titled Rival Verses, created with Daniel Johnston, highlighted decades of shared creative exchange and mutual inspiration. This added a second layer to the exhibition, pairing English’s broader symbolic world with a more personal artistic dialogue.

The surviving installation and exhibition images make this show especially useful for documenting the scale, density, and theatrical character of the presentation, as well as the breadth of paintings and sculptural works shown during the run.
