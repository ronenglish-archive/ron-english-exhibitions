Notes

Ron English X Secret Fresh was a holiday-season solo presentation in Manila that continued Ron English’s long-running relationship with Secret Fresh and its designer-toy-centered exhibition program.

The exhibition featured major Delusionville-related works, releases, and media attention, bringing English’s POPaganda imagery into direct conversation with Manila’s collector culture and pop-surreal art scene.

As a 2014 Secret Fresh presentation, the show is a useful record of English’s visibility in the Philippines and of the way his paintings, objects, and character-based imagery circulated within a gallery environment closely tied to toy and edition culture.
