Notes

The Other Side of Madison Avenue was a solo exhibition at South Texas College’s Pecan Campus Library Art Gallery, presenting works that critiqued advertising and mass-media aesthetics through Ron English’s POPaganda style.

Framed as an educational showcase, the exhibition offered students and visitors an introduction to English’s satirical treatment of advertising culture, corporate iconography, and the visual machinery of persuasion.

As a 2011 college-gallery presentation, it serves as a useful record of how English’s work circulated in academic and public-learning settings beyond the commercial gallery system.
