Notes

You Are Not Here was a solo museum exhibition at the International Museum of Art & Science in McAllen, Texas, exploring illusion, presence, and mass-media narratives within Ron English’s POPaganda universe.

The exhibition foregrounded paradox and perception, presenting English’s layered critique of commercial imagery in a museum-scale setting. This institutional context gave added weight to the tension in his work between spectacle, cultural familiarity, and visual destabilization.

As a 2011 museum presentation, the show is a useful record of English’s expanding visibility beyond gallery spaces and of how his POPaganda language translated into a broader public-art context.
