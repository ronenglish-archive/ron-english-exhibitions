Notes

Universal Grin was a solo exhibition in Old Montreal centered on Ron English’s evolving “Grin” iconography. The show expanded that recurring visual motif across paintings, editions, and sculptural objects within Galerie Matthew Namour’s contemporary art program.

By focusing on one of English’s most recognizable symbolic forms, the exhibition highlighted how a single pop-surreal device could shift across formats while still carrying his broader interests in branding, repetition, distortion, and cultural shorthand.

As a 2018 Montreal presentation, the exhibition is a useful record of English’s continued international gallery presence and of the way his Grin imagery functioned as a coherent exhibition theme in its own right.
