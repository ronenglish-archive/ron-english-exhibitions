Notes

English Translation Series presented Ron English’s paintings, sculptures, and designer-toy works in an immersive installation at Pop Life Global’s Q-Plex venue in Shenzhen.

The exhibition extended English’s POPaganda language into a presentation shaped around translation, adaptation, and circulation across cultural contexts, using a display environment that moved beyond a standard wall-based gallery format.

As a 2019 Shenzhen exhibition, it is a useful record of English’s continued visibility in Asia and of how his work functioned across painting, object-making, and collectible culture within a unified solo presentation.
