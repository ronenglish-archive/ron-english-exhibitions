Notes

POPaganda at Gallery @ The Falcon featured framed photographs and circus-style banners from Ron English’s Delusionville universe, bringing that performative visual world into the Hudson Valley during late 2017 and early 2018.

Rather than focusing only on conventional paintings, the exhibition highlighted banner-based and image-driven material tied to English’s broader theatrical and character-based practice. That emphasis gave the show a distinct atmosphere, closer to an immersive visual environment than a standard gallery presentation.

The New Year reception on January 6, 2018, marked the closing phase of the short-run exhibition and helps place the show within English’s wider exhibition activity leading into 2018.
