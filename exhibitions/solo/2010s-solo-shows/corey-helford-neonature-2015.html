Notes

Ron English NeoNature — We Are the New They opened Corey Helford Gallery’s new downtown Los Angeles space in late 2015. The exhibition introduced English’s “NeoNature” world through hybrid creatures, altered species, and symbolic animal imagery that pushed his Popaganda language toward ecological allegory.

The body of work framed mutation and evolution as both warning and fantasy, presenting a future shaped by human intervention, adaptation, and environmental pressure. In this setting, English’s familiar pop-surreal distortions took on a sharper biological and planetary dimension.

The surviving installation views make the exhibition especially useful for documenting the scale and visual density of the presentation, as well as the breadth of paintings and related imagery shown during the run.
