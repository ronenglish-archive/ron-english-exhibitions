Notes

Varnish Fine Art hosted Ron English’s 2005 solo exhibition Son of Pop, announced through an official showcard listing the run from May 28 to July 2, 2005, with an opening reception on June 2 and a private preview on May 28.

The exhibition foregrounded English’s investigations into American advertising, cartoon archetypes, and the cultural afterlife of pop icons. Contemporary coverage described the show as part of his expanding presence in West Coast art spaces, where satire and painterly precision worked side by side.

Period documentation also places Son of Pop within a broader 2005 record of English’s exhibition activity and ties it to the hybrid pop-culture figures and polished distortions that defined this phase of his work.
