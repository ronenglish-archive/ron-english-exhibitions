Notes

Held over Easter weekend in 2005, this two-day exhibition at Arte Vista: The Pop-Art Gallery introduced Ron English’s work to audiences in the Netherlands. The Dutch invitation notes that English officially opened the exhibition at 13:00 on Sunday, 27 March. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

The surviving promotional material highlights imagery associated with English’s early-2000s Popaganda output, including absinthe-themed motifs and reworked Marilyn imagery. These details place the exhibition within a period when his international visibility in pop-surreal and pop-art contexts was growing. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Although brief in duration, the exhibition is a useful record of English’s European exhibition activity and of the circulation of his work in specialized pop-art venues outside the United States. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
