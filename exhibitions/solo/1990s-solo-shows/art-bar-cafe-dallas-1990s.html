Notes

This early solo exhibition took place at Art Bar & Café, a Dallas venue associated with nightlife, alternative culture, and grassroots arts programming.

The surviving showcard identifies the evening as a dedicated Ron English presentation and notes that it was followed by a Club Clearview CD-release party for the Dallas band The Sutcliffes.

While modest in scale, the event helps document English’s presence in independent art spaces during the 1990s, especially in settings shaped by alternative music and countercultural scenes.
