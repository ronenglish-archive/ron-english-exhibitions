Notes

This invitation documents the grand opening of Sheer Magniloquent Pas, described on the card as “the evening art space in Chelsea,” at 142 West 26th Street in Manhattan. The event was scheduled for New Year’s Eve and featured the art of Ron English. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

The surviving card places the presentation within a nightlife-oriented exhibition setting rather than a conventional daytime gallery model. It also credits Baird Jones as host, linking the event to the downtown art and club network that shaped part of English’s public exhibition history during the 1990s. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

The Chelsea address and the description of the venue as an evening art space help situate this presentation within the neighborhood’s broader late-twentieth-century studio and gallery scene. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
