Notes

Held at the downtown nightclub Den of Thieves, this late-night Camel Lights–sponsored event showcased a selection of Ron English’s new giclée canvases.

During the mid-1990s, English was expanding the use of digital printing technologies in his practice and pairing them with hand-painted interventions. This compact solo presentation highlighted that experimental direction while placing his work in the center of New York’s nightlife scene, where fashion, advertising, and counterculture collided.

The surviving show card emphasizes the hybrid atmosphere of the event, pairing fine art with a nightclub setting and reflecting English’s tendency to bridge commercial imagery with artistic critique.
