Notes

Ron English presented a photographic exhibition at Thomas V. Robinson / Robinson Galleries in Houston as part of FotoFest ’90. The documented materials identify the exhibition as opening on February 11, 1990, with the artist present.

Contemporary coverage describes English’s photographs as combining actual people, hand-drawn cardboard figures, and flattened spatial elements. The same coverage notes that the exhibition included his series American Sideshows, placing the show within a body of work that explored staged illusion and photographic construction.

A Houston Post listing connected to the exhibition reproduces English’s 1989 C-print Out of the Frying Pan, helping confirm the venue and the public presentation of this work during FotoFest.
