Notes

Beyond the Groovy Planet is one of Ron English’s earliest documented solo exhibitions in New York. Held at Levlowe Gallery in 1980, the show was presented as “new stuff” by English.

The illustrated showcard suggests an early visual language built from surreal distortion, cartoon logic, and psychedelic imagery. Multi-headed figures, floating eyes, and exaggerated forms point toward themes that would later reappear in his more widely known Popaganda work.

The exhibition took place in the East Village on Friday, November 28, from 7 to 10 p.m., and the hand-drawn postcard reflects the underground downtown energy surrounding English’s early New York activity.
