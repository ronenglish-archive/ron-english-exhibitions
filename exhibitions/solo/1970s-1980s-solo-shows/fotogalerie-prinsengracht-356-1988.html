Notes

Ron English presented a photographic exhibition at Fotogalerie Prinsengracht 356 in Amsterdam in August 1988. The documented dates run from 11 August through 3 September, with a public opening on 13 August.

The exhibition announcement describes the work as engaging two aspects of photography at once: its ability to record reality and its ability to produce illusion. It also notes that the photographs were made without darkroom manipulation and presents the work as “straight” photography.

This show is an important record of English’s photographic practice during the late 1980s and helps document his exhibition activity in Europe during that period.
